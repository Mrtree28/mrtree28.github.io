STAR CORP INFORMATION TERMINAL

Out-of-Character Information

This website is a fictional worldbuilding project centered around "Star Corp," a database of anomalous entities, locations, and technologies.

It is heavily inspired by Minecraft-related media, especially classic creepypastas, as well as narrative-driven experiences similar to Minecraft: Story Mode.

The universe contains 26 known sites, each designated by a letter (A–Z). Not all sites are currently documented, leaving room for expansion.

Purpose of the Site

The goal of this project is to build a structured, collaborative universe where users can create and expand upon original ideas within the Star Corp setting.

Entries may include:

  • Entities (e.g. A-1, B-2)
  • Sites and locations
  • Technologies or artifacts
  • Stories and incident reports

Submission Guidelines

  • Create original content (do not copy existing creepypastas)
  • Keep a semi-serious, in-universe tone
  • Follow the structure of existing entries
  • Expand the world logically and creatively

All submissions may be reviewed, edited, or adapted before being added to the official database.

Notes

This is an out-of-character page meant to guide contributors. All other pages are written in-universe unless stated otherwise.
