STAR CORP FILE B-1

"The Hooded Death"

Status: NEAR CONTAINMENT

Overview

B-1, designated "The Hooded Death," is a cloaked humanoid entity with a concealed face, glowing red eyes, and a human-like structure.

Behavior

B-1 stalks individuals traveling toward the Far Lands, striking 1–5 hours before arrival.

It attacks by dragging victims into their own shadow, consuming the head instantly, then feeding on the rest over 3–4 weeks.

Weakness

The entity is highly vulnerable to intense light sources, including fire and weapon flashes.

Containment Progress

B-1 is close to containment using glowstone-based illumination and netherrack structures.

Notes

"Do not travel alone toward the Far Lands."
