STAR CORP FILE A-1

"Herobrine"

Containment Status: UNCONTAINED

Overview

A-1, designated "Herobrine," was first recorded during Incident X-15-2. The entity demonstrates awareness of STAR CORP operations and retains prior familiarity with the organization’s founder, "Notch."

Recorded Statement

"My own brother had forgotten about me in that horrible realm, and forced me to walk shackled with my own thoughts for a century, until YOU... decided to release my chains and let me free.

What he didn't realize is that I am now even more powerful than him... I allowed myself to meld with it and become even greater.

I AM A GOD NOW. I HAVE SURPASSED EVEN HIM.

I AM ICARUS IF HE WAS NOT CURSED TO BE WROUGHT FROM THE VERY SKIES THAT PRAISE HIM."

When questioned, "Notch" refused to comment and forcibly ended the interaction.

Description

A-1 appears to consist primarily of dense, anomalous fog. A humanoid form is occasionally visible within the mass, suggesting a possible structural core or weakness.

The fog induces intense hallucinations, typically manifesting as the subject’s worst fear. In a majority of cases, this fear takes the form of A-1 itself.

A-1 is capable of shaping and solidifying its fog into physical constructs. These constructs can be formed within biological targets, resulting in immediate and often fatal damage.

The entity displays sadistic behavior, frequently prolonging encounters to psychologically torment victims before termination.

Origin Theories

  • This universe
  • "The Foreverlands"

If A-1 gains access to the "Foreverlands," it is theorized that it could envelop the world in fog and induce widespread terror. Current behavior suggests this is not its immediate objective.

Status

A-1 remains uncontained.
