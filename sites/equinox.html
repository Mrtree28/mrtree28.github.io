Star Site Equinox

Overview

Star Site Equinox was the fifth site created by Starcorp and the first successful activation of the Reality Ladder.

Incident Report X-15-2 (First Contact)

The Reality Ladder was stirred to life as the Redstone Heart pulsed rapidly. A rift was formed successfully.

Moments later, a dense fog filled the room. Visibility dropped to near zero. A pair of glowing white eyes emerged from within.

The Site Director, Dr. Elanor, ordered all units to fire. All projectiles passed through the entity before the fog hardened into tendrils.

All personnel were eliminated except Dr. Elanor. The entity approached her and spoke:

"thank you"

Dr. Elanor survived and was recovered by a Task Force.
